简体中文

如果你在使用薪动力时遇到问题，或希望反馈建议，请通过上方邮箱联系我们。

常见问题

1. 小组件没有刷新怎么办？

请打开 App，确认月薪、计薪天数和上下班时间设置正确，然后点击“保存并刷新小组件”。如果仍未更新，可以尝试移除小组件后重新添加。

2. App 会上传我的工资数据吗？

不会。你的月薪、时间设置、计时数据和整蛊计数仅保存在设备本地，用于 App 和小组件展示。

3. 为什么工资进度和实际到账不完全一致？

App 根据你输入的月薪、每月计薪天数和工作时间进行估算，结果用于提供可视化参考，不代表真实工资发放记录。

4. 以后会加入更多小组件皮肤吗？

我们计划在后续版本中加入更多可选的小组件样式。核心工资进度功能会保持可用。

FAQ

1. What should I do if the widget does not refresh?

Please open the app, check that your monthly salary, paid working days, and work hours are set correctly, then tap “Save and Refresh Widget.” If it still does not update, try removing the widget and adding it again.

2. Does the app upload my salary data?

No. Your salary, time settings, timer records, and prank counters are stored only locally on your device for app and widget display.

3. Why does the salary progress not exactly match my actual paycheck?

The app estimates salary progress based on the monthly salary, paid working days, and working hours you enter. The result is for visual reference and motivation only, and does not represent actual payroll records.

4. Will more widget themes be added?

We plan to add more optional widget styles in future versions. The core salary progress feature will remain available.
