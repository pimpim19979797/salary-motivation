About the App

My Workday: Pay Widget is an iOS 17+ workday companion that turns your work schedule and compensation settings into estimated earnings progress, payday countdowns, Home Screen widgets, and optional work-mood check-ins.

Getting started

  1. Open Today and use the menu in the upper-right corner to configure your compensation, typical work schedule, and payday schedule.
  2. Open Widget Studio to choose an Earnings, Payday, or Work Mood widget style and customize the available colors, typography, and size.
  3. Tap Save & Apply, then add My Workday: Pay Widget from the iOS Home Screen widget gallery.

Frequently asked questions

Why has my widget not refreshed?

  1. Open the App and confirm your schedule and widget settings.
  2. Return to the widget editor and tap Save & Apply.
  3. Wait briefly for iOS to refresh the widget. Widget refresh timing is controlled by iOS and may not be immediate.
  4. If the widget still shows old content, remove it from the Home Screen and add it again.

Why does the earnings amount differ from my paycheck?

Earnings shown by the App are estimates based on the compensation and schedule information you enter. They may not include taxes, deductions, benefits, bonuses, payroll rounding, unpaid time, or employer-specific rules. The App is a personal visualization tool and is not a payroll record, pay stub, accounting service, or financial adviser.

How is the payday countdown calculated?

The App uses the pay frequency and next payday you configure, together with the date, time zone, and calendar on your device. If the countdown looks incorrect, check the payday schedule in the Today screen menu and confirm that your iPhone date and time zone are correct.

How do I hide earnings amounts on widgets?

Open Settings and enable the option to hide amounts. This privacy option affects supported earnings displays without deleting your compensation settings.

Where do I record my work mood?

Work Mood check-ins are recorded from Today. Choose one of the six moods, select the work factor that influenced it, and optionally add a note. You can review and delete entries in Mood History.

Does the App upload or sync my information?

No. The current version has no account or cloud sync. Compensation settings, schedules, mood records, notes, and widget preferences remain on your device. See the Privacy Policy for details.

What happens if I remove a widget or delete the App?

Removing a Home Screen widget does not delete the App’s settings or records. Deleting the App generally deletes its locally stored data. Because there is currently no cloud sync, deleted settings and mood history may not be recoverable after reinstalling.

Contacting support

To help us investigate an issue, include:

  • your iPhone or iPad model and iOS version;
  • the App version;
  • the widget family and style involved;
  • the steps that led to the issue; and
  • a screenshot, if it does not reveal sensitive information.

Please protect your privacy: do not email your exact compensation, private mood notes, passwords, payment information, or other sensitive personal information. You may redact screenshots before sending them.

Compatibility and accessibility

The current version supports iOS 17 and later and provides small and medium Home Screen widgets. If you encounter clipping, unreadable text, color-contrast problems, or another accessibility issue, please contact us with your device model, display settings, and a screenshot.
