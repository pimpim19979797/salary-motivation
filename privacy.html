My Workday: Pay Widget

Privacy Policy

Effective date and last updated: July 18, 2026

Support

Short version: My Workday: Pay Widget is designed to work without an account. The app does not transmit your compensation, work schedule, payday schedule, mood check-ins, notes, or widget settings to the developer or to an advertising or analytics service. This information is processed and stored on your device.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how My Workday: Pay Widget (the “App”) handles information. In this policy, “we,” “us,” and “our” refer to the developer of the App.

2. Information processed on your device

The App may process the following information when you choose to enter or configure it:

  • Compensation settings: pay basis, pay rate or salary amount, currency, overtime settings, and related earning preferences.
  • Work schedule: selected workdays and typical start and end times.
  • Payday schedule: pay frequency and next payday date.
  • Mood check-ins: the mood you select, the work factor associated with that mood, the date, and any optional note you add.
  • Widget preferences: selected widget styles, sizes, colors, typography, amount-privacy choices, and other presentation settings.
  • App preferences: appearance, onboarding state, and similar functional settings.

This information is stored locally in the App’s iOS storage and, where needed for Home Screen widgets, in an Apple App Group container shared only between the App and its widget extension. It is used to calculate estimates, render the App, and provide the widgets you configure.

3. Data we collect

We do not collect App data. For purposes of this policy, “collect” means transmitting information off your device so that we or a third party can access it. The App does not send the information listed above to our servers. The current version does not include developer-operated accounts, cloud sync, advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or tracking technologies.

We do not sell or share your App data, and we do not use it for advertising or cross-app tracking.

4. Device date, time, and calendar

The App uses the date, time, time zone, and calendar available on your device to calculate workday progress, payday timing, and dated mood history. The App does not request your precise or approximate location.

5. Apple services and the support website

Apple may process information when you download or use the App through services such as the App Store, iOS, and WidgetKit. Apple handles that information under its own privacy policies; we are not responsible for information Apple independently processes.

These Privacy and Support pages are intended to be hosted through GitHub Pages. GitHub may process limited technical information, such as IP addresses and request logs, when serving the pages. That processing is governed by the GitHub General Privacy Statement. These pages do not add analytics scripts, advertising pixels, or cookies.

6. Support communications

If you contact us for support, we receive the information you choose to provide, such as your email address, message, screenshots, and diagnostic details. We use it only to respond to your request, troubleshoot the issue, prevent abuse, and maintain appropriate support records. Please do not send sensitive compensation details or mood notes in a support request.

7. Retention and deletion

  • App settings and records remain on your device until you edit or delete them, reset the relevant feature, or delete the App.
  • Mood entries can be deleted from Mood History in the App.
  • Because the App does not operate a user account or cloud database, there is no server-side App profile for us to delete.
  • Support emails may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to resolve the request, maintain support records, comply with law, or protect our rights. You may ask us to delete a support conversation, subject to legal obligations.

Deleting the App generally removes its local data from the device. Removing a widget alone does not delete App data. Reinstalling the App may not restore prior settings or mood records because the current version does not provide cloud backup or sync.

8. Security

The App relies on iOS sandboxing and Apple’s App Group protections to isolate local information. No storage or security method can be guaranteed to be completely secure, so you should protect access to your device and avoid entering information you do not want stored there.

9. Children’s privacy

The App is a workplace utility and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the App.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the App changes. We will post the revised policy on this page and update the “last updated” date. If a future version introduces off-device data collection, accounts, analytics, advertising, or cloud sync, this policy and the App Store privacy disclosures will be updated before that use begins.
