简体中文

薪动力尊重并保护用户隐私。本隐私政策说明本 App 如何处理用户信息。

1. 我们收集哪些数据

薪动力不会收集、上传、出售或共享你的个人数据。

你在 App 中输入或产生的月薪、每月计薪天数、上下班时间、摸鱼计时、整蛊计数、显示模式等信息，仅保存在你的设备本地，用于 App 和小组件展示。

2. 数据是否会上传到服务器

不会。本 App 当前版本不提供账号登录、云同步、服务器存储、广告 SDK 或第三方分析 SDK。你的使用数据不会离开你的设备。

3. 小组件数据

为了让主屏幕小组件展示工资进度，App 会在本机共享存储中保存必要的设置和计算结果。这些数据仍然只保存在你的设备本地。

4. 内购与支付

当前版本不包含内购。如未来版本加入可选付费内容，支付过程将由 Apple 处理。本 App 不会获取你的银行卡号、支付密码或完整支付凭据。

5. 数据删除

你可以在 App 中修改设置，也可以通过删除 App 的方式删除保存在设备本地的数据。

6. 联系我们

如果你对本隐私政策有任何问题，请通过以下邮箱联系我们：blakeroy@163.com

English

Salary Motivation respects and protects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user information.

1. Data We Collect

Salary Motivation does not collect, upload, sell, or share your personal data.

Information you enter or generate in the app, such as monthly salary, paid working days per month, work start and end times, break timer records, prank counters, and display mode preferences, is stored only locally on your device and is used for app and widget display.

2. Server Uploads

No data is uploaded to our servers. The current version of the app does not provide account login, cloud sync, server storage, advertising SDKs, or third-party analytics SDKs. Your usage data does not leave your device.

3. Widget Data

To display salary progress in the Home Screen widget, the app stores necessary settings and calculation results in local shared storage on your device. This data remains stored only on your device.

4. In-App Purchases and Payments

The current version does not include in-app purchases. If optional paid features are added in a future version, payment processing will be handled by Apple. The app will not access your bank card number, payment password, or full payment credentials.

5. Data Deletion

You can change your settings in the app, or delete locally stored app data by deleting the app from your device.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at blakeroy@163.com.

This app is a salary progress visualization tool. Calculations are estimates for motivation and reference only, and do not represent actual payroll records.
